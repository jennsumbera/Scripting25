About

Fort McMurray is a beautiful place to be, affordable, and worthwhile.

Fort McMurray is my hometown where I was raised for the majority of my life. It is based in Northern Alberta and is most known for being the hub of the Oil Sands industry. Often people describe Fort Mac as an industrial resource-based, rugged, resilient, and natural community. It is a small city-town (municipality) that has definitely been growing and expanding as years go on. For example, there was the closure of a downtown Landmarks Cinema location which got redeveloped in another area in town for a new and improved movie theatre to fully bask in the movie-going experience. It is joyfully one of my favourite places in Fort Mac. The entire Eagle Ridge Boulevard has tons of fast food and restaurant locations, barbershops, eye centers, arcades, and bars all within walkable distance from each other. Convenient and affordable, and for the whole family!
