About

I am a 1st year Multimedia student at Lethbridge Polytechnic. Living my whole life in Canada, and having two immigrant parents both from different parts of the world, (Phillipines and Czech Republic) has taught me to truly embrace my roots, culture and background. Luckily I travel a lot, it's always nice to reunite with family, Especially being very family-oriented. I an also a member and advocate for LGBTQ+ communities and rights, and am passionate about animals and music!

Another one of my hobbies are watching a film whenever I get the chance, some of my favourite cinematic movies and film that are more nature related include "The Wild Robot", "Interstellar", and "Fantastic Mr. Fox". They are all beautiful and unique in their own ways, the animation is stunning, the production and soundtracks/scores are so beyond moving it will make you pause and think profoundly.

Being invested in the art of travel, nature and photgraphy, I hope to bring what I learn in this program with me anywhere I go in the world! Letting the inspiration flow while feel as connected and authentic as I could possibly be when it comes to my creations and overall path. Camping has also been a family outing we do any chance we get. It's what sparked that drive in me, the love I carry for nature itself, always felt in my element around trees specifically! A wave of safety, protection, and groundedness overcomes me, when I take time to meditate in the woods.
